My Project Title

Photography Portfolio for Nusra Jahan

Project: Web-based application for a photographer

Brief Description (Summary, Abstract): Nusra Jahan is a photography enthusiast who takes pictures of people on the Stony Brook University campus, including her friends and friends of friends who have seen her work and want to be photographed by her. Currently, she is posting her pictures on social media for her friends to see, but now she wishes to expand. She would like to take more portraits and perhaps be hired for specific occasions. A website would aid her in this endeavor because it would allow for anyone to see her work and to book a time for a session. The target audience for this website would be anyone who wishes to hire a photographer (perhaps some student organizations or even the university for sports events, for example) and also anybody that she has photographed that wishes to view the pictures from their session.
